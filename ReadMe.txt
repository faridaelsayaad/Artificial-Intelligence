Two search algorithims are applied: Breadth First Search and Depth First Search
These two algorithms are apllied on a maze
first start by chosing start cell
to choose start cell
click on the cell then press s 

second choose goal cell
to choose goal cell
click on the cell,then press g 

to start drawing the maze
click on the cell then press space

to erase a filled cell 
click on the cell and press d

After declaring the start,goal,and drawing the maze

to apply the breadth first search 
press on w
the visited and the fringe will be printed in the output

to apply the depth first search 
close the program and run again
press on t
the visited and the fringe will be printed in the output
